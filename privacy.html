Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1/22/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information in connection with our workforce staffing and communications platform.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Employment-related information (such as availability, assignments, and work history)
  • Communication preferences

Phone numbers are collected only when individuals voluntarily provide them as part of the hiring, onboarding, or work assignment process.

How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

  • Communicate about work assignments, shift confirmations, reminders, and operational updates
  • Provide staffing-related services to clients and workers
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Maintain records required for operational and compliance purposes

SMS messages are transactional and operational, not marketing.

SMS Communications

If you provide your phone number and opt in to SMS communications, you may receive text messages related to:

  • Job or shift offers
  • Shift confirmations or changes
  • Schedule reminders
  • Operational or support-related messages

Message frequency varies based on assignments and activity.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help deliver communications (such as SMS and email providers)
  • Clients involved in staffing assignments (limited to what is necessary)

All service providers are required to protect personal data.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Data Retention

Personal information is retained only as long as necessary for staffing operations, legal requirements, and legitimate business purposes.

Your Choices

  • You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any text message.
  • You may request assistance by replying HELP.
